Life at Diversify

As our name suggests, we value the diversity that great teams represent and embrace the different backgrounds, interests, passions, skills and experiences that make our team members uniquely themselves. We believe these are meaningful traits that promote work-life balance for all our teams to perform at the highest level.

So, we take a holistic approach to team engagement and performance and provide both our offshore workforce and our management teams with lots of opportunities to shine – in and out of the office.
Whether it’s through our monthly, themed team ‘huddle’ events that have ranged from beauty pageants, music festivals and country fairs, our friendly (but fierce!) inter-office gaming and sports competitions, annual social events, or just casual chats by the water cooler, we encourage our people to connect, laugh and support each other as an important aspect of their working life.
